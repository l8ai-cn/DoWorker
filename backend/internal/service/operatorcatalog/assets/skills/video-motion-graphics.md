# Video Motion Graphics

Design motion graphics that support comprehension rather than compete with the edit.

## Workflow

1. Identify the spoken or visual payoff each graphic must reinforce.
2. Choose the lightest suitable method: static card, image sequence, Remotion component, browser capture, or FFmpeg overlay.
3. Define entrance, readable hold, and exit durations. Land the main reveal on the relevant narration beat.
4. Reveal one information group at a time.
5. Keep typography, colors, spacing, and motion consistent with the supplied brand system.
6. Render overlays with alpha when they will be composited later.
7. Inspect the first frame, payoff frame, final hold, and composite result.

## Constraints

- Avoid linear easing for expressive motion.
- Hold a completed information state long enough to read.
- Keep essential text inside title-safe margins.
- Do not cover faces, product evidence, subtitles, or platform UI.
- Use the same frame rate and color assumptions as the master timeline.
